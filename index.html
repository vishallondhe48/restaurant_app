ABOUT US

        Restaurant Vesuvio, opened in 2003, is a dynamic, food driven restaurant that focuses on the elegance of cooking and a unique dining experience. This down-to-earth restaurant has been granted one Michelin Star since 2007. vesuvio's passion and dedication to food with its laidback integration gives customers an exciting evening every single time.The chefs innovate a well-thought daily menu using the best seasonal ingredients available. Customers choose the number of courses, the options are four to seven dishes.
